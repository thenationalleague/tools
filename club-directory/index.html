Style 1

Clean cards

Classic tool UI. Each person gets a small card in a 2-column grid within their section. Low visual noise, familiar pattern, works well on mobile where the grid collapses to 1 column. Best for quick skimming.

Style 2

Dense table

Spreadsheet-style layout with section dividers. Highest information density — you can see 20+ people without scrolling. Feels like a proper business tool. Best for power users who want to scan fast.

Style 3

Editorial

Magazine-style page with big display typography, serif for names, generous spacing. Feels premium and considered — closer to the printed handbook's character. Slower to scan, but every club page feels crafted. More vertical scrolling.

Style 4

Role-first

Role is the anchor, rendered as a left-hand pill, names to the right. Reads like a job board or org chart. Strongest when the thing you care about is "who holds role X" rather than "who is person Y".
